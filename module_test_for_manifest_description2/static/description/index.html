Objectives & Key Results - OKRs

Help you implement and execute OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) in your organizations

Key Features

Define objectives & key results for the company, department, employees by quarter.

Measure objective achievement automatically from its key result's points.

Visualize objectives and key results hierarchy with OKR chart.

Ready for other applications to extend (e.g. Projects, Timesheet, Gamification, etc).

Benefits

Clear objective linkage: Connect personal, department, and company objectives, helping everyone work towards the same goal.
Enhance individual contribution: Help employees understand their role, increase motivation, and improve work efficiency.
Long-term strategy: Support building and tracking long-term strategies, helping sustainable growth.
Easy progress tracking: Automatically measure and update goal progress based on key results, enhancing management efficiency.
Visual data: OKR diagrams provide transparency in progress, aiding quick and accurate decision-making.

Define Objectives & Key Results

You can easily define OKR nodes and link them together. The final result of the company will be the small objectives of each department. Similarly, the key results of the department will be the smaller objectives of the members within that department. Therefore, each personal objective will be a key result for a company objective.

Measure objective automatically from its key result's points

The OKR module will automatically calculate the achievement of an objective based on the points of its key results.

Layout Feature 3

The layout of the OKR module is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate. The module is divided into several sections, each of which serves a specific purpose. The main sections include the Objective and Key Result tables, as well as a calendar view and a dashboard.
